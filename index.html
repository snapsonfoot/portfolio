⟶ How to add your photos

  1. Put your JPEG/WebP images in a folder called images/ next to this HTML file.
  2. In the PHOTOS array below (inside the <script> tag), replace each src with your filename, e.g. "images/my-photo.jpg".
  3. Update the title and category for each photo.
  4. Add or remove objects in the array to match how many photos you have.
  5. Change Your Name in the header to your actual name.
  6. Delete this instructions box when you're done.
